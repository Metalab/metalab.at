Visit Us!

Metalab is located at Rathausstraße 6 in the first district of Vienna, Austria, Europe.

Vienna is not suitable for cars, so please come here using public transportation (in ascending order of walking distance):

  • Metro lines U2 and U5, tram line 2 at station Rathaus
  • Tram lines U2Z, 1, 71, D at station Parlament
  • Tram line 46 at station Auerspergstraße
  • Metro line U3 at station Volkstheater
  • Tram lines 37, 38, 40, 41, 42, 43, 44 station Schottentor

Alternatively, there are also a few bicycle parking spots in the immediate vicinity.
